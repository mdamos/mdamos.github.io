Getting Started

To use InterAACt, you need to have a Google account. You can use this account to sign
into the application.

Login to InterAACt

  1. Press the "Login with Google" button.
  2. Choose a Google account to sign in with. If none are listed, you either don't have any
    Google accounts or are not currently signed into any on your device.
  3. Once you're logged in, your screen orientation will change to landscape mode.

Contacts

Viewing your contacts

  1. On the home page, press the "View Contacts" button
  2. A list of your contacts should appear.

Create a new contact

  1. In the upper right-hand corner of your contact list, locate and press the "Create
    Contact" button.
  2. Enter the contact's name & email address.
  3. Save the contact by pressing the floppy disk icon on the top right-hand corner of
    the screen.
  4. You will be directed back to the home page, where you can press "View Contacts" to
    see your new contact.

Deleting a contact

  1. Press on the contact you want to delete.
  2. In the top right-hand corner, select the three dot icon.
  3. Press "" and you will be directed to the application's home page.
  4. The contact should be removed from your contact list.

Scrolling

  1. Use your finger or the black arrows to scroll up or down through your contact list.
  2. Press the "To Bottom" to scroll down to the bottom of your contact list.
  3. Press the "To Top" to scroll down to the top of your contact list.

Calling

Placing calls

  1. Choose a contact from your contact list to call by pressing on the square.
  2. Press the green button with phone icon.
  3. Make sure your media volume at about 60% or higher. For example: (insert example pic)
  4. Choose an communication method to use during the call:
    • Symbol board* - An array of buttons with symbols that,
      when pressed, will display words that communicate what the symbol is portraying.
      Each button displays what will be converted to speech
      below the symbol image.
    • Standard keyboard* - Your device's keyboard that can
      be used to construct sentences to send using text-to-speech.
    • No keyboard - For those who don't want to use text-to-speech.

      • *Use the "Play" button to send the phrase/sentence you've put together
      using the communication method you've chosen. Use the "Clear" button to erase all
      of the words displayed in the text box.
